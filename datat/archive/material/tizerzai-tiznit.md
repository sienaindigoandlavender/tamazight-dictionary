The triangular form of the *tizerzai* (fibula) serves a dual purpose of structural utility and metaphysical shielding.

### Materiality and Craft
The base plate is carved from heavy coin silver, incised with deep geometric grooves to hold the yellow and green enamel powder before firing. The central boss is fixed with a raw fragment of Mediterranean red coral.
